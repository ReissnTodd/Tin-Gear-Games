Golem Township
Golem Township is the first game released by Tin Gear Games, in which you manage a small but growing clade of golems as you seek to build a township and establish yourself in a desolate fantasy medieval setting. In the game you will be able to:
  • Collect a variety of golems to assist you
  • Fend off a variety of bandits and magical beasts
  • Create a bustling township and enrich yourself
  • Become A God
The goal for the game is to be a much more automation focused take on a similar idea to Stardew Valley
